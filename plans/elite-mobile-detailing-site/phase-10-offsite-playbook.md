# Phase 10 — Off-site SEO Playbook (owner / post-launch)

**Goal:** The ranking work outside the codebase — for a mobile detailer this drives most of
the local visibility. Owner-driven and ongoing.
**Depends on:** Phase 9 (site live)
**Status:** ⬜ Not started
**Spec refs:** `SEO_STRATEGY.md` Part B + Measurement + §A8 (roadmap).

## Tasks

- [ ] **P10-T1 — Google Business Profile**
  - Create/claim; set up as a service-area business (hide address); primary category
    **"Car detailing service"** + secondaries; exact NAP + local number; service areas; services
    with prices; 200–500-char description; attributes; real professional photos.
- [ ] **P10-T2 — Reviews engine**
  - Post-job one-tap GBP review link; target 3–5 genuine reviews/month; respond to every review
    within 24–48h; encourage customers to mention service + city + vehicle.
- [ ] **P10-T3 — Citations**
  - Identical NAP across GBP → Apple → Bing → Yelp → Foursquare → BBB/YP/Manta/Chamber; audit for drift.
- [ ] **P10-T4 — Local links + E-E-A-T**
  - Partnerships (apartments/offices/dealers), local sponsorships, real team/credentials, insured/certified.
- [ ] **P10-T5 — Measurement cadence**
  - Monthly review of Search Console, GBP insights, and a Local Falcon geo-grid for Map-Pack rank.
- [ ] **P10-T6 — Roadmap triggers**
  - When real per-area content/reviews exist, build priority city pages
    (`/mobile-car-detailing-plano-tx`, unique content — avoid doorway pages) and a blog/content engine
    (cost guides, ceramic-vs-wax, seasonal). See `SEO_STRATEGY.md` §A8.

## Done when
GBP is live and fully optimized, the citation set is NAP-consistent, the review flow is running,
and monthly tracking is in place. (City pages/blog remain future, trigger-based.)
