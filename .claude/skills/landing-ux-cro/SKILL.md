# Landing UX & CRO

Goal: maximize qualified conversions across THREE paths — **Book** (Cal.com), **Quote**
(form), **Call** (`tel:`). Target 5%→8–10% (local-service average ~2%).

## Rules

- Keep all three CTAs available; sticky mobile bar `[Book] [Call]` within thumb's reach.
- Hero must carry: benefit headline, the canonical trust strip
  ("100% self-contained · Same-day slots · Insured & vetted"), and a single optimized hero
  IMAGE (the LCP element — not a video, not the before/after slider). COLD-START: no Google
  reviews yet → show that trust strip + the satisfaction guarantee, NOT a fabricated star
  rating; add the "★ + review count" row once real GBP reviews exist.
- Move proof UP: Our Work gallery (and real testimonials, once collected) before the booking ask.
- Pricing: keep it transparent (an edge — competitors hide it); Good/Better/Best tiers;
  "Most Popular" on tier B; ceramic = TIERED (3-yr / 9-yr) + "Request a Quote" (needs inspection).
  The size selector doubles as an INSTANT ESTIMATOR (package + size → exact price + "Book this").
- Copy: concise, premium, US English; consistent CTA wording ("Book Your Detail", "Get a
  Free Quote", "Call"). Never ship internal/placeholder language (e.g. "high-margin").
- Payment framing: card / contactless / Apple Pay first (cash welcome) — never "cash first".
- Motion subtle; never block the primary action; honor `prefers-reduced-motion`.
